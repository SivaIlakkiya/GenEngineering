About Us

Gen engineering is a growing Company, inaugurated in 2013 and our management team has around 20 years' experience in structural steel design & detailing. Our crew members are extensive experienced in project management, Steel fabrication & erection and involved in projects ranges from 0.1 ton plate works to 20,000 tons Coal power plants.

Quality Policy

We are devoted to continuosly improve to enhance quality of our Engineering services and ensure a long lasting growing customers relationship and excel ever increasing levels of customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To achieve excellence in Engineering services by having satisfied Customers through Consistent quality deliverables, promptness, and innovative cost effective solutions.

Our Vision

To be recognized in the global market as a world class engineering service provider ensuring customer delight through quality, integrity, responsibility and reliability.

Our Values

We are extremely quality conscious. The quality of our work is guaranteed by the implementation of strict detailing procedures and commitment to excellence.

Our Services

Estimation & Take Off

We offer Material take off for main steel & miscellenous steel including detailing schedule & cost per clint needs.

  • 1.Material Summary list.
  • 2.Area & Linear meter details wherever required.
  • 3.Non standard formed member shapes drawings.
  • 4.3D Wire frame model.
  • 5.Preliminary RFI.

Steel Detailing

Gen Engineering provides comprehensive solutions for structural steel detailing services in AISC, OSHA, BS, Candian, European, South african & Indian Standards at competitive prices with high quality. We have expert detailers with vast and rich experience in Detailing, Fabrication & Erection.

  • 1.Advanced BOM
  • 2.Non Standard formed member shapes drawings.
  • 3.Part Drawings.
  • 4.Assembly Drawings.
